Gutters

Gutters generally belong on containers, but they can also be applied to blocks.

  • .gutter
  • .gutter-lg

Containers

Containers belong inside of regions. They have a max width and float clearing and can have a gutter class.

<div class="region-blue" style="margin-bottom:20px;overflow: hidden;">
  <div class="container gutter">
    <p style="color:#fff;">container, gutter</p>
  </div>
</div>

Regions

Regions are generally top-level elements that have background colors & images, but they can also be used inside of containers or grid elements.

<div class="region-teal region--modifier">
  <div class="container gutter">
    <p style="color:#fff;text-align:center;">Nulla vitae elit libero, a pharetra augue.</p>
  </div>
</div>

Region Space

Add vertical spacing to regions (in blue).

<div class="region-blue region--space-sm">
  <div class="container gutter" style="background: #F6F5F2">
    <p style="text-align:center;">Nulla vitae elit libero, a pharetra augue.</p>
  </div>
</div>
<br>
<div class="region--space-md">
  <div class="container gutter" style="background: #F6F5F2">
    <p style="text-align:center;">Nulla vitae elit libero, a pharetra augue.</p>
  </div>
</div>
